Independent, experience-led, technology & Services advisory for companies in the Lloyd's & London Specialty Insurance Market.

Mojio & Co provides independent, experience-led advisory services to technology and services providers seeking to better understand market dynamics, identify emerging opportunities, and design go-to-market strategies that resonate with Managing Agents, insurers, brokers, and MGAs in the Lloyd's & London Specialty Insurance market.

Typically, engagements focus on three areas: market insight, proposition shaping, and commercial and marketing strategy, with selective execution support available where it adds tangible value. Additional services are available including assistance with sales leadership and hire selection, as well as vendor and system evaluation.

Alongside client work, the firm actively supports early-stage technology ventures through structured pro-bono mentoring, reflecting a long-term commitment to strengthening the quality and effectiveness of innovation entering the market.
