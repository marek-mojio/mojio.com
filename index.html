Independent, experience-led, technology & services advisory for companies in the Lloyd’s & London Specialty Insurance Market.

Mojio & Co provides independent, experience-led advisory services to technology and services providers in the Lloyd's and London Specilaty Insurance Market. We help companies seeking to better understand market dynamics, identify emerging opportunities, and help design technology adoption and go-to-market strategies that resonate with Managing Agents, insurers, brokers, and MGAs.

Typically, engagements focus on three areas: market insight, proposition shaping, and commercial and marketing strategy. We also offer execution support where we can add tangible value. Further capbilities include assistance with sales leadership and hire selection, as well as vendor and system evaluation.

Alongside client work, the firm actively supports the Market's early-stage technology ventures, most noteably those taking their journey through the Lloyd's Lab. Mojio & Co has provided this structured pro-bono mentoring since 2022, reflecting our commitment to strengthening the quality and effectiveness of innovation entering the market.
